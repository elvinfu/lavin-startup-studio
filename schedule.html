What will the week look like?
Studio and Social Events

Have a chance to meet fellow students interested in entrepreneurship and learn more about the mini startup venture you'll be creating with your fellow teammates. Start talking about your ideas and get to know your team if you went through our team-matching process!

Special Speaker Sessions

Interested in hearing about entrepreneurship from those with first-hand experience? Come listen to our experienced speakers, ranging from those that have exited from startups, are currently engaged in a startup venture, or are involved in other ways, such as venture capital.

Workshop Events

We'll offer a variety of workshop events in order to give you opportunities to work on parts of the startup process you are interested in, such as ideation, prototyping, and more.


Final Pitch (Top 10 Teams)

For only the top 10 teams (decided based on deliverables), members of each team will bring their startup materials they've created throughout the week as well as give a startup pitch. Teams will pitch to a set of judges and/or venture capitalists who'll help decide which team's startup has the most potential!
